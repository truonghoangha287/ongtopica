# Movers — Places and Directions (new at this level)

## Places
| Word | Example sentence |
|------|-----------------|
| airport | We go to the airport to catch a plane. |
| bakery | She buys bread from the bakery. |
| beach | We swim at the beach in summer. |
| bookshop | He buys books at the bookshop. |
| café | They have coffee at the café. |
| campsite | We sleep in tents at the campsite. |
| castle | The old castle is on the hill. |
| city | She lives in a big city. |
| countryside | They go to the countryside at weekends. |
| farm | The cows live on the farm. |
| hotel | We stay at a hotel on holiday. |
| island | The island is surrounded by water. |
| lake | They swim in the lake. |
| library | She borrows books from the library. |
| market | They buy fruit at the market. |
| mountain | They climb the mountain. |
| museum | The museum has many old paintings. |
| petrol station | Dad stops at the petrol station. |
| pharmacy | She buys medicine at the pharmacy. |
| pool | They swim in the pool. |
| post office | She sends a letter at the post office. |
| restaurant | We eat at a restaurant on Sundays. |
| river | Fish swim in the river. |
| road | Be careful when crossing the road. |
| sea | I love swimming in the sea. |
| sports centre | She plays tennis at the sports centre. |
| station | Take the train from the station. |
| supermarket | We buy food at the supermarket. |
| village | They live in a small village. |
| waterfall | The waterfall is very beautiful. |

## Direction Words
| Word | Example sentence |
|------|-----------------|
| above | The bird flies above the clouds. |
| across | She walks across the bridge. |
| along | Walk along the road. |
| below | The fish swim below the water. |
| corner | Turn left at the corner. |
| direction | Which direction is the school? |
| far | Is the park far from here? |
| left | Turn left at the traffic lights. |
| near | The school is near my house. |
| next to | The bank is next to the café. |
| north / south / east / west | Go north for two kilometres. |
| opposite | The supermarket is opposite the park. |
| right | Turn right at the shop. |
| straight | Go straight along this road. |
| turn | Turn left at the corner. |
