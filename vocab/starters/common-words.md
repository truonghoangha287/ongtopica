# Starters — Common Words and Phrases

## Question Words
| Word | Example |
|------|---------|
| how | How are you? |
| what | What is this? |
| where | Where is my bag? |
| who | Who is that woman? |
| why | Why are you sad? |

## Determiners and Pronouns
| Word | Example |
|------|---------|
| a / an | I have a cat. She has an apple. |
| the | The dog is big. |
| this | What is this? |
| that | Look at that bird! |
| it | It is a book. |
| he | He is my brother. |
| she | She is my sister. |
| they | They are my friends. |
| we | We are happy. |
| I | I am seven years old. |
| you | You are very tall. |
| my | This is my bag. |
| his | That is his book. |
| her | This is her doll. |
| their | Those are their toys. |
| our | This is our classroom. |

## Prepositions
| Word | Example |
|------|---------|
| in | The cat is in the box. |
| on | The book is on the table. |
| under | The ball is under the chair. |
| next to | The lamp is next to the bed. |
| behind | The dog is behind the sofa. |
| in front of | The car is in front of the house. |
| between | The cat is between the boxes. |
| at | She is at school. |

## Conjunctions
| Word | Example |
|------|---------|
| and | I like cats and dogs. |
| but | I like cake but not vegetables. |
| or | Do you want juice or water? |

## Common Phrases
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Hello! | greeting |
| Goodbye! / Bye! | farewell |
| Thank you. | gratitude |
| Please. | polite request |
| Sorry. | apology |
| Yes. | affirmative |
| No. | negative |
| Here you are. | offering something |
| Good morning! | morning greeting |
| Good afternoon! | afternoon greeting |
| Good evening! | evening greeting |
| Good night! | bedtime farewell |
| How are you? | asking about feelings |
| I'm fine, thank you. | polite answer |
| I don't know. | expressing uncertainty |
| I like / I don't like | expressing preferences |
| My name is ... | introducing yourself |
| I am ... years old. | stating age |
| Look! | drawing attention |
| Sit down! / Stand up! | classroom instructions |
