# Cambridge English Qualifications for Kids — Vocabulary

Organized by exam level, then by topic.

| Folder | Level | CEFR | Approx. vocabulary |
|--------|-------|------|-------------------|
| `starters/` | Pre-A1 Starters | Pre-A1 | ~300 words |
| `movers/` | A1 Movers | A1 | ~450 new words (+Starters) |
| `flyers/` | A2 Flyers | A2 | ~700 new words (+Movers) |
| `a2-key-for-schools/` | A2 Key for Schools | A2 | ~1200 words (own list) |

**Note:** Movers and Flyers lists contain only words *new at that level* — students are expected to know all words from previous levels. A2 Key for Schools has its own standalone list (overlaps heavily with Flyers).
